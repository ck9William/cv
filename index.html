About Me.

Why I entered the gaming industry

When I first time opened The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim on my computer, I found the external goal of my life. I will enter the game industry and bring the shock and touch that I feel at this moment to more people. I was deeply attracted by the story and the freedom in the game. To have a better game experience, I started to research how to install plug-ins, MODs. After that, I started to learn 3D modeling, and import the characters I made into the game. Finally, I made my own game. Games are a paradise for players on earth. Games allow people to sit in front of the computer table and experience places and people that might be inaccessible in their lifetime.
